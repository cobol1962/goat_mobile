GOAT100 or Greatest of All Time 100 is a dynamic card list of the top 100 artists in a chosen category. The votes are solely depended on users voting to finally answer the peoples question... Who is the greatest rapper of all time?

This is for the culture to showcase who really our fan favourites and who deserves respect for their artistry. From classic artists to emerging talent, we showcase the artists and their music that takes them to the top of the peoples chart!
No tampering nor influence from anyone, the list is the truth!
Request new artists at the bottom of the lists to have new talent showcase or classic artists ready to reclaim their position!
Every week, Artists who are in the bottom 10 are liable for delegation so new requested artists can take their place!
So to keep your favourite artist in the list, you must vote!
Users can only vote once on each card, each day to avoid spamming!
You must register to make a vote which is just a simple account which can be made through Facebook, Twitter and Google+ accounts.
Each board has social links, Spotify and Apple Music links aswell as our own catered information on each artist and their accolades. There are also unique comment boards for each artist to chat away!

Shout out to all the music publications, radio stations, podcasts and online debates across the world that have kept the converstation going!

  • 🐐 = GOAT 100 - The top 100 rappers of all time voted by you!
  • 🇬🇧 = UK - The top current artists voted by users!
  • More lists coming soon!
